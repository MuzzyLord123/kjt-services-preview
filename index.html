Good to know

Frequently asked questions

The things customers usually ask before booking. Anything else — just ask.

Is the quote really free?

Yes — completely. We'll visit, look at the job properly, and give you a clear written price. There's no call-out fee for quotes and no obligation to go ahead.

How quickly can you start?

Small jobs can often be fitted in within a week or two; larger projects are usually booked a few weeks ahead. You'll get a realistic start date with your quote — and we stick to it.

Do you take on small jobs?

Absolutely — no job too small. A sticking door, a shelf, a single room to paint: the small stuff is where most of our long-term customers started.

Are you insured?

Yes — public liability insurance is in place for every job. Happy to show you the certificate before work starts.

How and when do I pay?

For most jobs, nothing is due until the work is finished and you're happy — then payment by bank transfer or cash. Larger projects may be split into agreed stages, all set out in writing with your quote.

Is there VAT on top of the quote?

No — the price on your written quote is the full amount you pay. We're not VAT-registered, so nothing gets added on top and there are no surprises at the end.

Do you supply materials, or should I?

Either works. We can supply and collect everything (itemised in your quote), or fit materials you've already bought — whichever suits you and your budget.

Got a job that needs doing?

Tell us what you need and we'll get back to you with a free, no-obligation quote — usually within one working day.
